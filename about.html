About

Who I am

Just another sci-fi junkie raised on the 1984 Macintosh who can't stand to write about myself. Born in New York City, educated on the internet, and exercised on my bike. Since I can't go to space (allergic to space dust), I decided to orbit the planet at low altitude by heading East until I hit America. Here I am again.

What I do

I like to solve problems by applying design-based thinking to the technology available. Most problems are simpler than we realize, and most proposed solutions are more complex than necessary. I find that happy middle-ground where products thrive and businesses grow.

What I know

Give me any design, and I can prototype it in HTML and SASS, with a dash of Javascript. Show me an interface, and I will refine it to a shining polish with nary a pixel missed. Describe a product's context and goal, and I will boil down the features to an MVP that will get things rolling.

Where I want to be

A small startup that wants to build a great product to solve an interesting problem. I want to work with people who aren't afraid to experiment and take risks, trust the people working alongside them, and have a deep respect for the power of small teams working closely together towards a common goal.
