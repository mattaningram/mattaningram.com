Responsibilities

MechoSystems' products are complex feats of engineering, and it was my job to convey the benefits, features, or technical functionality of these products in clear and simple formats so that anyone from designers and architects to engineers and electricians could understand and learn from them. I designed product catalogs, manuals, technical briefs, and marketing materials involving detailed retouching of photos, 2D and 3D illustrations, product packaging layout, and maintaining consistency with the company style guide.
