Created and designed an app to plan and share events with friends. Built a **playful, distinctly branded visual identity** with a custom **themable design system** that let groups personalize the experience. Focused on a **no-accounts-necessary, privacy-first approach** to keep things lightweight and trustworthy, with **polished micro-interactions** and a UI that's fun to use from the first tap.
